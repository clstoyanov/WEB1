Tom Clancy's The Division 2 is an online action role-playing video game developed by Massive Entertainment and published by Ubisoft.
Initial release date: March 15, 2019
Engine: Snowdrop
Publisher: Ubisoft
Developers: Massive Entertainment, Ubisoft Reflections
Platforms: PlayStation 4, Xbox One, Microsoft Windows
Genres: Action role-playing game, Survival game, Third-person shooter
