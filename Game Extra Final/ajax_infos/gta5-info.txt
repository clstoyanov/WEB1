Grand Theft Auto V is an action-adventure video game developed by Rockstar North and published by Rockstar Games. It was released in September 2013 for PlayStation 3 and Xbox 360, in November 2014 for PlayStation 4 and Xbox One, and in April 2015 for Microsoft Windows.
Initial release date: September 17, 2013
Awards: VGX Award for Game of the Year, etc.
Platforms: PlayStation 4, PlayStation 3, Xbox 360, Xbox One, Microsoft Windows
Rating: Mature
Developers: Rockstar Games, Rockstar North, etc.
Did you know: "Grand Theft Auto V" is the third-best-selling video game (100,000,000 units sold).
