Resident Evil 2 is a survival horror game developed and published by Capcom and released for the PlayStation in 1998.
Initial release date: January 21, 1998
Writer: Noboru Sugimura
Mode: Single-player video game
Platforms: PlayStation, IBM PC compatible, Nintendo 64, GameCube, Microsoft Windows, Dreamcast, Game.com
Developers: Capcom, Rockstar San Diego, Tiger Electronics, Factor 5, Capcom Production Studio 3
Designers: Shinji Mikami, Hideki Kamiya
